## Channel Economics

### Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) Estimates

| Channel | Effort Hours | Expected Users | Effective CPA |
|---------|-------------|----------------|---------------|
| Awesome lists | 10 hrs | 100 users | 0.1 hr/user |
| Discord soft launch | 20 hrs | 75 users | 0.27 hr/user |
| HN post | 8 hrs | 100 users | 0.08 hr/user |
| Web skill browser | 80 hrs | 2,000 users (yr 1) | 0.04 hr/user |
| VS Code extension | 100 hrs | 1,000 users | 0.1 hr/user |
| Author virality | 60 hrs (dashboards) | 1,500 users (yr 1) | 0.04 hr/user |

**Most efficient:** Web skill browser and author virality (lowest ongoing effort per user)

**Least efficient:** Discord/Reddit (requires ongoing engagement)

## Key Insight: Investable vs. Uncertain Channels

From Growth Engineer review:

| Channel Category | Characteristic | Our Approach |
|-----------------|----------------|--------------|
| **Investable** | Predictable, repeatable, compounds | Primary focus |
| **Uncertain** | Lottery-like, one-time spike | Nice-to-have |

### Investable Channels (Primary Focus)

1. **Awesome lists** - Predictable acceptance rate (~60%), steady trickle
2. **SEO/Web browser** - Compounds over time, measurable
3. **Author virality** - Aligned incentives, scales with ecosystem
4. **VS Code extension** - Marketplace discovery, ratings-driven

### Uncertain Channels (Secondary)

1. **HN front page** - 10% of posts get meaningful traction
2. **Viral Twitter moment** - Unpredictable
3. **Anthropic endorsement** - Low probability, high impact
