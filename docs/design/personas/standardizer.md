# The Standardizer

> **Navigation**: [Design Index](../index.md) > [Personas](./index.md) > Standardizer

**Archetype:** The Team Lead Seeking Consistency

> *"I need everyone using the same tools, configured the same way."*

---

## Demographics

- Tech lead or engineering manager
- Responsible for team productivity and standards
- 8-15 years experience
- Makes or influences tool decisions for team

---

## Goals

| Functional | Emotional |
|------------|-----------|
| Curate approved skill sets | Feel in control of team quality |
| Ensure consistent Claude behavior | Confidence in team alignment |
| Onboard new developers efficiently | Pride in smooth operations |
| Reduce "works on my machine" issues | Relief from debugging variance |

---

## Fears and Anxieties

- Being blamed for bad tool recommendations
- Team members using conflicting skills
- Security vulnerabilities in unapproved tools
- Complexity that only they understand

---

## Current Behavior and Workarounds

- Maintains internal wiki of approved tools
- Personally tests skills before recommending
- Creates onboarding checklists for new team members
- Struggles to enforce consistency across team

---

## Trust Triggers (What Builds Confidence)

- Enterprise readiness indicators
- Security audit information
- Clear versioning and stability commitment
- Team usage analytics
- Easy rollout and enforcement mechanisms

---

## Delight Triggers (What Creates Joy)

- New team member productive on day one
- Seeing team-wide adoption of a recommendation
- Reduced support tickets about tool configuration
- Stakeholder recognition for smooth operations

---

## Preferred Entry Points

1. **Web report** shareable with stakeholders
2. **GitHub Action** for PR recommendations
3. **Team registry** for centralized management
4. **Terminal** for personal evaluation

---

## Red Flags That Cause Abandonment

- No way to create team-wide configurations
- Individual user focus with no team features
- Lack of audit trails
- No enterprise security features roadmap
- Recommendations that vary by team member

---

## Design Implications

### For Discovery Features
- Support team skill registries
- Show adoption rates across team
- Provide shareable reports and recommendations

### For Search
- Filter by "enterprise-ready" or "security-audited"
- Show skills used by similar teams/companies
- Surface stability and support commitment

### For Recommendations
- Recommend based on team stack, not individual
- Show team-wide adoption potential
- Flag skills with team-compatibility concerns

### For Installation
- Support team-wide rollout commands
- Provide configuration export/import
- Track installation across team members

### For Ongoing Experience
- Dashboard showing team skill usage
- Alerts for version mismatches across team
- Compliance and security update notifications
